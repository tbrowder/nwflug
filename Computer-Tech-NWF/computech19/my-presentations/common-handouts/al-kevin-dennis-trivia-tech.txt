Al Holzschuh wanted a question to stump his son-in-law.

Here are some from Kevin Dennis:

Trivia

Q: What name did Linuz Torvalds originally want to use?

A: Not Linux, Freax => combination of freak and free.

Tech

Q: How do you enable TCP fast open?

A: Modify sysctl parameter:

   net.ipv4.tcp_fastopen = 3

Q: How do you adjust the page cache swapping?

A: Change vm.swappiness setting in

   /proc/sys/vm/swappiness
